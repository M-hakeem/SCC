ABOUT US

The Sector Skills Council has a mandate under the NSQF to promote Skill development in the Building sector. Therefore, its aims are to develop, establish, standardize and sustain Industry Competency Frameworks, Skills Levels, Occupational Standards, and oversee the development and delivery of capacity, investment and skilling outcomes which shall meet or exceed Construction Industry expectations.

STRATEGY

Universally, a Sector Skills Council contributes to a skill system on the premise of, but not limited to the following: • development of National Occupational Standards for the appropriate skills, • the design and approval of Apprenticeship frameworks for the absorption of persons into the skills, • brokering Sector Skills Agreements and • Creating Sector Qualification Strategies./p>
